## Life
**id:** `life`
**Title (ZH):** 
**Medium:** 陶瓷装置 / Ceramic installation
**Dimensions:** 40 × 23 × 41 cm
**Year:** 2024

**descriptionEn:**
> 
In the summer of ’24 and spring of ’25, I immersed myself in Jingdezhen – whether dwelling in nature, visiting ruins or working in the studio, I was constantly accompanied by ducks, dogs and peacocks, and occasionally visited by insects, snakes and toads. Last summer was my first visit to Jingdezhen – a city shrouded in swirling dust and wrapped in clay. I am beyond grateful for having met Mentor Li. In our time together – neither too brief nor too long – he not only taught me Jingdezhen’s traditional porcelain craft but also took me on a journey through the city’s ancient kiln ruins, clay pits, and working furnaces, showing a deeper, more essential side of the city’s heritage. Starlit skies shimmering like the milky way, the thrill of discovering clutches of eggs and witnessing hatchlings emerge, wandering through spring fields, mountain woods, streams and boulders – these are just some of Jingdezhen’s indelible fragments. 

Due to uneven shrinkage inside the kiln, not leaving a single piece intact. At that moment, I experience a strange sense of calm, as if recognizing the inevitability of fate and impermanence of things. 

The finished, collapsed house seemed like a predestined fable – abandoned, imperfect, and lost. In the eyes of the era and society, it was deemed no longer ‘correct’ enough, surfeit, no longer needed. In truth, the life of the house resembles human life: from birth to death, stuck within an inescapable cycle of repetition and return.

**descriptionZh:**
> 
24年夏天和25年的春天，我泡在了景德镇.废墟、自然、工作室的各种鸡鸭狗孔雀、时不时出现的昆虫、小蛇、蟾蜍围绕着我。去年是我头一次到景德镇，第一印象是尘土飞扬，泥土包裹着整个城市.

我很感激遇见李老师，不长不短的时间里不仅教我景德镇传统瓷器的技艺，同时跑了很多古遗址、泥料厂、窑厂等等，带我了解到景德镇更深、更原始的一面.银河般闪烁的星空，发现一窝窝鸡蛋，小鸡出壳的惊喜，在春天的田野、山林、溪水、岩石之间游荡......是景德镇难忘的片段.

陶瓷创作的源起，是基于我2024年3月看到的孤独、废弃房子的情感冲动开始，为完成脑海中的这件作品，我把自己投入到陌生的环境中.尽管花了大力气，房子的框架还是在最后 ，因为炉内收缩不均衡的原因塌的不成样子，没有一块完好.
那一刻我却反常的平静，认识到命运的注定和无常.

最后烧制的坍塌的房子像一个注定的寓言，它是被遗弃的、不完美的、失落的，对于时代和社会来说它已经不够“正确”，从而不被需要.房子的一生其实和人的一生很像，从出生到死亡，逃避不了的轮回和反复.

## Spider Web
**id:** `spider-web`
**Title (ZH):** 《蜘蛛网》
**Medium:** 布面丙烯 / Acrylic on canvas
**Dimensions:** 40 × 40 cm
**Year:** 2024

**descriptionEn:**
>
 Before attending university in Suzhou, I have always lived in Tiantai. As a degree of distance accumulated between myself and my hometown, I began to observe Tiantai’s natural features and folk customs from a relatively more detached perspective. 

I enjoy exploring Tiantai’s wilderness, mountain monasteries, old alleyways and forgotten spaces…

喜欢探索天台的荒野、山寺、老巷和废墟……

古物和故人唤醒我最远的记忆，和最深的情感.这样的习惯也影响我，每到一个地方时，去观察那些微弱的力量，找到与内心有共鸣的画面.

## 水面
**id:** `water-surface`
**Title (ZH):** 水面
**Medium:** 瓷板画 / Porcelain painting
**Dimensions:** TODO
**Year:** TODO

**descriptionEn:**
> 
  This collection of works was born from the soil and smoky warmth of Jingdezhen in spring.
  The spark of inspiration hides in those fleeting fragments of light and shadow...
  It is the flowering tree at the studio door, whose fruit — if it will ever come — remains a quiet mystery;
  It is the temperature woven between the warm afternoon sun of early spring and the cold night wind that has not yet fully retreated;
  It is the stillness felt riding an electric scooter through fields ablaze with rapeseed blossoms, watching farmers at their work, as chimney smoke and the evening glow rise together into the sky;
  It is the joy of shared adventure — climbing the hill to dig for bamboo shoots, gathering curious pieces of driftwood and moss-covered stones along the stream, and waiting together, breathless with delight, for tiny chicks to crack their way into the world.

**descriptionZh:**
> 
  这组作品，诞生于景德镇春天的泥土与烟火气。
  灵光藏在那些细碎的光影里……
  是工作室门口那棵不知能否结出桃子的花树；
  是初春午后的暖阳与夜里尚未散尽的冷风交织出的温度；
  是骑着电瓶车，穿过开满油菜花的田野，看农人劳作，炊烟与晚霞一同升起的平静感受；
  是一起冒险；上山挖笋、捡拾溪边奇特的木头与长满青苔的石头、一起等待小鸡破壳出生的欣喜.

**id:** `porcelain-painting-1`
**Title (ZH):** 瓷板画 1
**Medium:** 瓷板画 / Porcelain painting
**Dimensions:** TODO
**Year:** TODO

**descriptionEn:**
> 
  This collection of works was born from the soil and smoky warmth of Jingdezhen in spring.
  The spark of inspiration hides in those fleeting fragments of light and shadow...
  It is the flowering tree at the studio door, whose fruit — if it will ever come — remains a quiet mystery;
  It is the temperature woven between the warm afternoon sun of early spring and the cold night wind that has not yet fully retreated;
  It is the stillness felt riding an electric scooter through fields ablaze with rapeseed blossoms, watching farmers at their work, as chimney smoke and the evening glow rise together into the sky;
  It is the joy of shared adventure — climbing the hill to dig for bamboo shoots, gathering curious pieces of driftwood and moss-covered stones along the stream, and waiting together, breathless with delight, for tiny chicks to crack their way into the world.

**descriptionZh:**
> 
  这组作品，诞生于景德镇春天的泥土与烟火气。
  灵光藏在那些细碎的光影里……
  是工作室门口那棵不知能否结出桃子的花树；
  是初春午后的暖阳与夜里尚未散尽的冷风交织出的温度；
  是骑着电瓶车，穿过开满油菜花的田野，看农人劳作，炊烟与晚霞一同升起的平静感受；
  是一起冒险；上山挖笋、捡拾溪边奇特的木头与长满青苔的石头、一起等待小鸡破壳出生的欣喜.
