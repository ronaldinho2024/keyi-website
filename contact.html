Fan Keyi is a Chinese artist from Tiantai, China. From 2022 to 2025, she received a Master's degree fron the China Academy of Art, majoring in Composite Printmaking. She currently lives and works in Hangzhou.

Her work spans a range of media including canvas, ceramics, paper, wood, and stone. Rooted in personal lived experience and emotion, her practice explores her relationship with life and the natural world. She seeks to let the softness and sensibility of femininity collide and grow against the roughness and sharpness of the wild — a response to the many absurdities and violences of the world we inhabit today.
